About Us

Welcome to Boon News—your trusted source for insightful, timely, and balanced news in Nigeria. Founded in 2007 by News Outline Limited, Boon News is dedicated to providing in-depth coverage and analysis on topics that matter most to Nigerians, fostering an informed, engaged, and empowered community. .


Our mission

At Boon News, our mission is simple yet powerful: to empower the Nigerian public with factual and reliable news. We believe that accurate information is the foundation of a strong community and a thriving democracy, and we are committed to upholding the highest standards of journalism.

What We Cover

Development

Our development coverage dives deep into Nigeria’s growth stories, from infrastructure advancements to emerging technologies and economic policies that shape the nation's future. We spotlight the projects, leaders, and initiatives driving change across Nigeria, highlighting the efforts that contribute to sustainable progress.

Social Benefits

We are passionate about bringing attention to stories that impact the lives of everyday Nigerians. Our reporting on social benefits sheds light on health, education, employment, and welfare initiatives designed to uplift individuals and families, making a real difference in communities nationwide.

Community Value

Boon News places a special focus on community stories, celebrating the resilience, diversity, and spirit of Nigerian people. From grassroots projects to local heroes, our community value segment showcases the power of collective action and the impact of local efforts in building a stronger, more connected Nigeria.

Politics and Governance

Stay informed on the latest in Nigerian politics, governance, and public policy. Our dedicated team provides objective and comprehensive analysis on political events and decisions that shape our country, helping readers stay engaged with the democratic process

Culture and Lifestyle

Nigeria’s rich culture is a core part of who we are. In our culture and lifestyle section, we explore the vibrant traditions, modern trends, and diverse expressions that define Nigerian identity, including arts, entertainment, fashion, and more.

Technology and Innovation .

As Nigeria becomes a growing hub for tech and innovation, we bring you news on the latest advancements and their implications. From startup success stories to government initiatives in the digital space, our tech coverage keeps readers on the pulse of Nigeria's role in the global tech landscape

Global Affairs

Our world is increasingly interconnected. Boon News extends beyond Nigeria’s borders to provide insights on global events, trends, and issues that have an impact on our nation, offering Nigerian perspectives on the international stage.

Our Commitment

Boon News is committed to upholding values of honesty, inclusivity, and excellence. We believe that a well-informed public is essential to a thriving nation, and our team of skilled journalists and editors work tirelessly to bring you stories that educate, inspire, and resonate. We aim to be your go-to platform for news, a place where you can find trustworthy information and connect with issues that matter.

Thank you for making Boon News part of your journey to stay informed and engaged.
